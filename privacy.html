Overview

We collect only the information necessary to provide roofing inspections, estimates, and services. We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers who help us operate our business (for example, scheduling, CRM, email/SMS communications, or advertising analytics), subject to appropriate safeguards.

This policy is intended to address common requirements under Canadian, U.S., and other privacy laws (including PIPEDA and certain U.S. state laws such as CCPA/CPRA). Your specific obligations may vary; this is not legal advice.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact & Identifiers: name, phone number, email (if provided), service address, and preferred contact method.
  • Project Details: property type, roofing needs, photos (if you send them), and notes you provide.
  • Lead Sources: submissions via Facebook/Instagram Lead Ads, our website forms, phone calls, and email.
  • Device & Usage (online): IP address, device/browser data, page views, clicks, and approximate location derived from IP (via analytics/cookies—see Cookies).
  • Communications: call recordings/voicemails (if applicable), texts, emails, and messages with us.

Sensitive data: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Please do not provide it.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide roofing inspections, estimates, and services you request.
  • Schedule appointments, confirm details, and provide updates (by phone, text, or email).
  • Respond to inquiries and deliver customer support.
  • Send service reminders or promotions (with your consent where required by law).
  • Improve our website, ads, and services (analytics and performance measurement).
  • Detect, prevent, and address security or fraud issues; comply with legal obligations.

By submitting a lead form, you agree we may contact you at the phone number and any email you provide for scheduling and follow‑up. Where required by law, we obtain your express consent before sending marketing messages. You can opt out at any time.

Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Contract/steps prior to contract (e.g., providing an estimate).
  • Consent (e.g., marketing communications; certain cookies/analytics).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving services, preventing fraud) balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligations (e.g., tax, accounting, compliance).

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers/contractors: CRM/scheduling, email/SMS providers, cloud hosting, analytics/ad platforms (e.g., Meta), and subcontractors assisting with your project.
  • Professional advisors: accountants, auditors, insurers, or lawyers, under confidentiality.
  • Authorities: when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.
  • Business transfers: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may transfer under equivalent protections.

We require service providers to use your data only to perform services for us and to protect it appropriately.

4. Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above: generally for the duration of your request/relationship plus a reasonable period (for example, up to 24 months), or as required by law. We delete or de‑identify data when no longer needed.

5. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal information.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time.
  • Opt out of the sale/share of personal information and targeted advertising (where applicable).
  • Portability (receive your data in a usable format).
  • Lodge a complaint with your privacy regulator/supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us by phone at 705 903 7663. We will verify your request and respond within the time required by law.

6. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our site, measure performance, and understand campaign effectiveness. Analytics/ad technologies (including Meta) may collect or receive information from our site and use it to provide measurement services and targeted ads. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some cookies are essential to site operation.

7. Security

We use reasonable physical, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; risks remain. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International transfers

We may process and store information in Canada, the United States, or other countries. When transferring data, we apply appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law (for example, contracts with service providers).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date above.

When you submit a lead form on Facebook or Instagram, Meta provides us with the information you choose to share (for example, name, phone, address, and any email). We use this information solely to respond to your roofing inquiry, schedule inspections, provide estimates, and—if you consent—send relevant service updates or promotions. SMS/Text Message Terms

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive calls and text messages from Endura Roofing related to your inquiry, scheduling, and service updates. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to a text from us; for help, reply HELP or call 705 903 7663.
