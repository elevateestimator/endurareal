Metal Roofing FAQ

Is metal roofing noisy when it rains?

No. With proper underlayment and attic insulation, it’s no louder than shingles.

Can you install over existing shingles?

Sometimes, yes. We’ll inspect decking and ventilation to decide what’s best.

Will a metal roof increase home value?

Often yes. Longevity and energy efficiency are major selling points.
