Buying Tips

Standing seam vs ribbed steel — which should I choose?

Standing seam hides the fasteners for a hyper‑clean, long‑life aesthetic. Ribbed steel is rugged, proven, and cost‑efficient. Both are excellent—your home and goals decide the winner.

Can you help me match siding and soffit to the roof?

Yes. We’ll propose finish palettes that make the whole home feel cohesive and premium.
